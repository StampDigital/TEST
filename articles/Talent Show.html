Talent Show

Reviewed by Harrison Pow-Jones


To raise money for Children of Choba, 9F organised a talent show on the 25th of April. A good audience turned out as the 4 acts showed off their talents. First up was Jack Laffey, 9C, who stepped up to perform as there were only 3 other acts, and played the guitar to the song Imagine by John Lennon, unfortunately the guitar wasn't tuned so it did sound a bit off but all credit to him for stepping up to do it and help out.


Next was Jack Salty, 7F, who sang 'See You Again' by Wiz Khalifa and Charlie Puth, the crowd really got involved with his act and thoroughly enjoyed it and at the end he received a very loud applause. Act number three was also in year 7, who sung 7 years by Lucas Graham which everyone enjoyed. The final act of the day sung 'Lush Life' by Zara Larsson which also received a good applause. Well done to 9F for organising a fun and entertaining which raised lots of money for the Children of Choba campaign.
