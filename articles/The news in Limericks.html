The war on ISIS


There was a young girl who joined ISIS
Whose parents didn't care in the slightest.
To Syria she went,
Her philosophy bent,
She really wasn't the brightest.

Then refugees fled from the scene
To England's rich land of green.
The fight 'gainst ISIS
Had led to a crisis.
Shunning migrants is clearly obscene.

Shootings then happened in France
Without motive at first glance.
Then Paris responded:
Syria - they bombed it.
ISIS didn’t stand a chance.

A vote for bombings came hither
To make the Syrians quiver.
All in favour say ‘aye’,
‘ISIS will die!’ -
Relations began to wither.
